Marry Me Chicken

This chicken has totally changed my life! I have made it four times in the past couple months and doesn't seem to ever get old. Please write me and let me know if you love this chicken, too!

Ingredients
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup Sun dried tomatoes
  • 2 tbps olive oil
  • Fresh basil
Directions
  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Marinate the chicken in the olive oil and sundred tomatoes
  3. Put the chicken in the oven and bake for 25 minutes
